# Links to share (students, faculty, admin)

Replace the host with your production domain (e.g. **`https://lab-record-system.vercel.app`**).

| Who | URL |
|-----|-----|
| **Students** (main app) | `https://<your-host>/` or `/login` |
| **Faculty** | `https://<your-host>/faculty/login` |
| **Admin** | `https://<your-host>/admin/login` |

**Mobile:** There is no ⌘ / Ctrl+Shift+L on phones. Faculty and admins should open **`/faculty/login`** or **`/admin/login`** directly, or use **Student login → “Open staff login options”** on the student screen.

**Desktop (student login only):** **⌘+Shift+L** (Mac) or **Ctrl+Shift+L** (Windows/Linux) toggles the same staff links panel.
